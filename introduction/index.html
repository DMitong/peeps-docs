Why Peeps?

Existing social media platforms struggle to balance user privacy, security, and a rewarding user experience. They also suffer from:

  • Centralized control: User data is often stored and managed by centralized entities, raising concerns about privacy breaches and potential misuse.
  • Limited monetization for creators: Traditional social media platforms offer limited avenues for creators to directly monetize their content, hindering their ability to build sustainable careers.
  • Inauthentic interactions: The rise of bots and fake accounts can create a disingenuous online environment, diminishing the quality of social interaction.
  • Limited engagement: Social media platforms can prioritize algorithms and ads over fostering genuine connections between users, leading to a decline in user engagement.

Peeps aims to address these issues by leveraging blockchain technology and innovative features to create a secure, rewarding, and engaging social media experience.
